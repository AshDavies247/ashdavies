Garnish are an award-winning communications agency with an unparalleled knowledge and enthusiasm for the drinks industry.

We know what it takes to set-up a brand, how to distribute the product, the constraints of small to medium sized brands, and the difficulties of truly making an impact in a busy market.

Working with some of the best brands globally available, Garnish have produced a variety of PR/Media campaigns, marketing initiatives, large events and innovative social media campaigns.

From Diageo to start-up brands, the Garnish team has a wealth of experience with both agency and in house and is integral to every campaign that Garnish run.

Since the launch Garnish have won many awards and plaudits for the work and impact they have had on the drinks industry.
